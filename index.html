Tutorial

Click on the counters (incl. Turns and Coin Star) and it increases by the amount specified at the top, which can be changed by either clicking on them or pressing the respective keyboard button (0 for 10).
By holding Ctrl or checking the Minus Checkbox at the top it subtracts everything instead.
By clicking on the characters it gives or takes them the coin star from the character that was clicked on.

The "Generate & Copy" button outputs all counters as text and copies it (this can be configured in the settings).
The "Save" and "Reload" buttons save and reload all counters. Not the characters and not settings, only counters.
